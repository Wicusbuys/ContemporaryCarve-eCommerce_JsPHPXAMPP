Crafting a legacy

OUR STORY

CONTEMPORARY CARVE JOURNEY

When Willem Buys, a talented craftsman from South Africa, embarked on a design exchange in Australia, little did he know that it would pave the way for the humble beginnings of Contemporary Carve, a small woodworking shop. Driven by a passion for timeless furniture, an unwavering commitment to innovation, and a deep desire to elevate South African design on a global platform, Willem returned to Johannesburg with a vision that would shape the journey of his modest woodworking shop.

Through meticulous planning and honing their craft, Contemporary Carve proudly unveiled their inaugural collection of exquisite furniture at the local 100% Design Showcase. The crowning achievement arrived when their masterpiece, the Coronation bench, received the prestigious 'Best Furniture Design' award, providing a glimmer of recognition for the small woodworking shop. The impact of this accolade reverberated, propelling Contemporary Carve onto a broader stage. The Coronation bench found a cherished home with Karen Roos of Babylonstoren, while the support of esteemed design legend Gregor Jenkins served as a guiding light, opening doors to new possibilities.

Fuelled by an unwavering dedication to innovation, Contemporary Carve continued to refine their craft, expanding their offerings within their modest retail and collectible ranges. Nestled in the vibrant West Rand of Johannesburg, the woodworking shop experienced gradual growth, fostering a close-knit team of passionate artisans. Over time, accolades and recognition trickled in, as their pieces found places in local galleries, private residences, and respected institutions. From modest exhibitions to partnerships with emerging designers, Contemporary Carve carved out a niche for itself, showcasing their meticulous craftsmanship and artistic vision.

Today, their modest retail and trade offerings grace the interiors of diverse homes, boutique hotels, cozy offices, and charming local eateries. Collaborations with like-minded artisans remain at the heart of Contemporary Carve's ethos, propelling their pursuit of innovation within their intimate woodworking shop. While their story is still unfolding, Willem Buys and the team at Contemporary Carve remain steadfast, driven by an unwavering desire to push boundaries, set new standards, and etch their mark in the world of South African design, one exquisite piece at a time.

THE SHOP

OUR WOODWORKING HEART

Contemporary Carve, based in Johannesburg's West Rand, seamlessly blends personal touch with technology. Our team has grown from five to 60 in five years, leaving a positive mark on our staff and South Africa. Through apprenticeships combining CNC technology and handcrafted techniques, we provide entry points to the carpentry trade. We prioritize supporting our team, including hiring from a nearby facility for the mentally handicapped. Our commitment to community transformation is reflected in the quality and energy of our end products.
